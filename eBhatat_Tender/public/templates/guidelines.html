Tender Portal Guidelines

Official instructions and procedures for Vendors, Departments and Government Authorities

Vendor Guidelines
  • Register on the portal with valid business documents.
  • Complete profile with GST, PAN, and company details.
  • Download tender documents carefully.
  • Submit technical and financial bids before deadline.
  • Pay EMD (Earnest Money Deposit) if applicable.
  • Attend pre-bid meeting (if required).
  • Track application status from dashboard.
  • Maintain compliance with procurement rules.
Download Vendor Manual
Department Guidelines
  • Create tender with complete and clear specifications.
  • Upload BOQ, technical documents and drawings.
  • Set transparent eligibility criteria.
  • Define submission and closing dates properly.
  • Evaluate bids based on approved procedure.
  • Publish corrigendum if required.
  • Declare results officially through portal.
  • Maintain audit transparency.
Download Department Manual
Government Guidelines
  • Ensure transparency and fairness in procurement.
  • Follow national procurement policy framework.
  • Promote equal opportunity to vendors.
  • Maintain digital audit records.
  • Prevent corruption and malpractice.
  • Monitor compliance with financial rules.
  • Ensure timely contract award and execution.
  • Maintain public accountability.
