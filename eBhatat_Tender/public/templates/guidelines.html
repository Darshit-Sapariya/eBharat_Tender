Department Guidelines

For government departments & offices

  • Create tenders with complete and clear specifications
  • Upload BOQ, technical documents and drawings
  • Set transparent and fair eligibility criteria
  • Define submission and closing dates precisely
  • Evaluate bids strictly on the approved procedure
  • Publish corrigendum through the portal if required
  • Declare results officially through the portal
  • Maintain full audit trail and transparency

Government Guidelines

For authorities & policy enforcement

  • Ensure transparency and fairness in all procurement
  • Follow the national procurement policy framework
  • Promote equal opportunity to all eligible vendors
  • Maintain complete digital audit records
  • Prevent corruption and malpractice at all levels
  • Monitor compliance with financial rules and norms
  • Ensure timely contract award and execution
  • Maintain public accountability in all decisions
