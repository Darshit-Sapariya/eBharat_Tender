Mandatory KYC Re-verification for All Registered Vendors — Deadline: 31 March 2026

All registered vendors are hereby instructed to complete KYC re-verification by 31st March 2026. Failure to comply will result in temporary suspension of bidding privileges. This is a one-time mandatory compliance requirement per the updated procurement policy circular dated 01 March 2026.

Instructions for Vendors

  • Log in to your vendor portal → My Profile → KYC Verification
  • Upload valid business certificate, GST certificate, and PAN (PDF/JPG, max 5MB each)
  • Ensure all documents are self-attested and clearly legible
  • Submit form and await approval within 3–5 working days
  • Contact kyc@ebharattender.gov.in for any issues
